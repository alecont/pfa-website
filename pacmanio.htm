PACMANIO

PFA collective PACMANIO project explores our connection with videogames. We want to bring videogames images, colors, sounds and memories into our physical world!

Inspiration

① Our own experience making and playing videogames

② NYC MoMA videogame art installation, with their unique and subtle way to analyze videogames as art

③ Bring outsized objects in the open world like Claes Oldenburg and Coosje van Bruggen did

Phase #1 — Building PACMANIO
