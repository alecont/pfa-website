PO10DIY

We love the work and ideas from teenange engineering. Their Pocket Operators (or POs) are now 10 years old (nuts!). This is our entry in the PO10DIY contest — but really just a modest tribute to their work and inspiration!

① The cassette tape. That's how we first experienced music. It was portable, you could copy it easily and you put your own music and mixes on it 🤘

② The PO almost fits in a classic cassette case (uncanny!)

③ So we made a special case and a special J-card for our (and your) Pocket Operator. Carry it around in the case! Jot down your song titles and stories on the J-card!

④ Thanks to teenage engineering for all these ideas and beautiful things! To 10 more years! 🎉
